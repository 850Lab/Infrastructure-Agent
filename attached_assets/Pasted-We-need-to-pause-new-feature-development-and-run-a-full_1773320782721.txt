AUDIT RULES

3. Do not mark something complete unless:

   - it survives refresh/re-entry
   - it handles empty/error states
   - it persists correctly
   - it avoids duplicate or conflicting writes
   - it behaves correctly during multi-company operator use
4. Flag all hardcoded values, placeholder logic, fake reasoning, approximate KPI math, and brittle assumptions.
5. Prefer truth over appearance.
